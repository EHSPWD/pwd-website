Rod Wave, the stage name of Rodarius Marcell Green, is a Florida-born rapper, singer, and songwriter who blends hip-hop and R&B to create a soulful style. He's considered a leader of the "soul trap" subgenre, which combines soulful melodies with modern beats. Wave's emotive songwriting and rich vocals draw on personal experiences, including challenges from his upbringing, to express his struggles and triumphs.
