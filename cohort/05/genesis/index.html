Some Facts About Dominican Republic

Dominican Republic is the location of the frist European city in the Americas and has the highest peak in the caribbean. Santo Domingo, The capital, Was the first European settlement in the Amercias, Founded in 1496. The Dominican Flag is distinctive for being the only one in the world that contains a bible on it. The national dance and music styles of merengue and bachata and dembow originated in the Dominican Republic. The country us the only place in the world where Larimar, a rare and beautiuful gemstone, is minded. Baseball and basketball are the most popular sport, and the Dominican Republic is leading producer of Major League Baseball players.
