What is Weird Gun Game?

Weird Gun Game is a popular Roblox, first-person shooter also known as (FPS) that stands out from more conventional games with its unusual and highly customizable weapon-crafting system. Instead of picking from a fixed arsenal, players are given a workshop where they can mix and match parts from different weapons, such as barrels, stocks, sights, grips, and magazines.This system encourages players to experiment and get creative, leading to chaotic and unpredictable arena matches.

How to play!

Weird Gun Game on Roblox is a fast-paced FPS also known as (First-Person Shooter) where you customize your own chaotic firearms before jumping into the arena. Instead of using preset weapons, your primary objective is to create unique, effective—or even intentionally bizarre—weapons by mixing and matching different parts. In the pre-game lobby, you can access the build system to assemble a gun using six main components: a barrel, core, magazine, sight, grip, and stock. Each part influences your weapon's stats, including fire rate, damage, recoil, and reload speed, allowing for a vast number of strategic combinations.
