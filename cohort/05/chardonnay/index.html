About Kuroko no basket

Kuroko's Basketball (黒子のバスケ, Kuroko no Basuke)is a Japanese manga and anime series created by Tadatoshi Fujimaki. It centers on a high school basketball team's quest to win the national tournament, featuring both realistic gameplay and characters with "superpower-like" basketball abilities.

Some characters from Kuroko no basket are based off playing styles from famous Nba players

Some characters that are based off nba players are Aomine who is based off Kobe Bryant and Allen iverson and Murasakibara who is based off Shaquille O'Neal

About The Generation of Miracles

The Generation of Miracles (Kiseki no Sedai) is a legendary group of five exceptionally talented basketball players from Teikō Junior High, who won the national championships for three consecutive years. Upon graduating, they dispersed to different high schools, becoming rivals.

There are 5 key members in the Generation of Miracles. Daiki Aomine: A prodigious player known for his streetball-style play and speed. Ryota Kise: A player who can copy other players' skills. Shintaro Midorima: A player with an incredibly accurate three-point shot. Atsushi Murasakibara: A player with immense physical strength and height, making him a formidable defensive player. Seijuro Akashi: The captain of the team, known for his exceptional leadership and unique ability to control the game. Tetsuya Kuroko: A player who specializes in misdirection and passing, a "phantom sixth man"
