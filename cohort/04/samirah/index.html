Boston is the capital of Massachusetts and a major cultural and financial hub of New England, known for its rich American history, world-class universities, and sports culture. It is the site of many "firsts" in the U.S., including the first public park (Boston Common, 1634), the first public school (1635), and the first subway system (1897). The city also has a diverse economy that includes finance, higher education, and medicine.
